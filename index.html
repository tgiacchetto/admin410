History

DHP was founded by Mary and Jacob Parrot in 1971 right after Jacob got back from serving in the Vietnam War. Mary and Jacob opened a modest store at 8 Dover Road in Durham which was just a few blocks from their home. In those days DHP focused primarily on dog and cat supplies.

In 1992, after beginning to expand their offerings to include apparel, accessories and fish supplies, they opened a second store at 46 Main Street in the heart of Durham. Mary and Jacob have since retired and DHP is now owned and operated by their son, Peter, and his wife Patty.

DHP now offers one of the largest selections of pet supplies in the region.

In 2018, DHP opened a third location in downtown Portsmouth, New Hampshire.

Why DHP?

Durham House of Pets (DHP) is a full-service, family run, pet store with two convenient locations in Durham, New Hamphire, and a third location in Portsmouth, New Hampshire.

We offer a full range of food, supplies, apparel and accessories for dogs, cats, and fish. We carry many name brands of dog and cat food, at incredibly low prices. We also carry live fish, tropical and fresh water, in store, for immediate take home.

We hear time and time again from customers about the outstanding attention our employees povide those customers. Since we rely on repeat business, we focus on customer service and believe we are second to none in this area. We are happy to say that we have many long standing customers and a number of our employees have been with the company for many years.
