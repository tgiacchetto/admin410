Return Policies

We at DHP accept returns provided such products are regular priced and unopened items in the original packaging within 15 days
of your purchase with receipt or proof of purchase. Sale items are non-refundable. Unfortunately, if 15 days or more have
passed since your purchase, we cannot offer you a refund or an exchange. Upon receipt of the returned item, we will fully
examine it and notify you via email, within a reasonable period of time, whether you are entitled to a return. If you are entitled
to a return, we will refund your purchase price and a credit will automatically be applied to your
original method of payment. To follow-up on the status of your return, please contact us at 800-555-PETS.

Delivery

We typically use business couriers such as FedEx© and UPS© to ship our products. The cost for shipping shall be
borne by the customer, unless otherwise written, based upon the products purchased and the delivery location. In the event you
select to upgrade your shipping method for faster delivery (such as Second Day or Overnight), your order must be received and
credit authorization cleared by 9:00 p.m. ET or your order may not be processed until the following business day.
Business days are Monday-Friday, excluding federal holidays within the United States. No live animals will be shipped.

Financing

A convenient way for customers to purchase pet supplies is to apply for a DPH Credit Card. If you are at least 18 years
of age, you can obtain a DPH Credit Card and use it to purchase any store merchandise at any DPH store. One of the
additional benefits of using the DPH card is from time to time, we offer no interest promotions with equal monthly payment
options. That means, you can purchase your favorite products and take your time paying for them. See store associate for details.

Special Financing Offer

For all DPH Credit Card holders, for purchases totaling $500 and up, DPH is pleased to offer 0% APR for 6 months with equal
payments. Eligible purchases will be charged no interest from date of initial purchase until the balance is paid in full.
This offer will only be available for a limited time. See store associate for details and additional offers. Interest will
be charged to your account from the purchase date if the purchase balance is not paid in full within 6 months.
